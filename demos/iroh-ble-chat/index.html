Before we start

BlewChat is a toy.

It’s an experimental demo for the iroh-ble-transport crate — a way to watch the BLE radio path light up, not a messenger.

Not end-to-end encrypted. The protocol is unaudited. Don’t send anything you wouldn’t post publicly.

Bluetooth permission is required — please allow it when prompted, or nothing will happen.

Reset app?

This will delete your identity key, known peers, and nickname, then restart the app as if freshly installed. Messages already received will also be cleared. This cannot be undone.
